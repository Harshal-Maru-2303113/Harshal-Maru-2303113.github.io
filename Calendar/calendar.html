    IMPORTANT DATES

      CLIMATE DATES

      • 1.Earth Hour : On the last Saturday of March at 8:30 PM (local time), people worldwide turn off non-essential lights for one hour to promote energy conservation and climate action
      • 2.World Water Day : Observed on March 22, this day highlights the importance of freshwater resources and encourages water conservation and sustainable management.
      • 3.Earth Day : Celebrated on April 22, Earth Day focuses on environmental protection, conservation, and global awareness.
      • 4.World Environment Day : Held on June 5, this day emphasizes environmental action, biodiversity, and ecosystem preservation.
      • 5.World Oceans Day : Recognized on June 8, it promotes ocean health, marine conservation, and sustainable practices.
      • 6.International Day for Biological Diversity : May 22 aims to raise awareness about biodiversity loss and the need for conservation.
      • 7.World Wildlife Day : Observed on March 3, it celebrates the diversity of wildlife and raises awareness about endangered species.
      • 8.International Day of Forests : March 21 focuses on forest conservation, reforestation, and sustainable forest management.
      • 9.World Wetlands Day : February 2 highlights wetland ecosystems and their importance for biodiversity and climate regulation.
      • 10.Global Recycling Day : March 18 encourages recycling practices and waste reduction.
